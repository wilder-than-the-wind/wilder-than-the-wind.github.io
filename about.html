Our Values

Sharing Our Values Through Art

I have a passion to create with lots of colour and fluidity and function. My art is affordable and vivid. My classes are easy to follow and you will have this knowledge for life, as I send all my students home with a handout of the subject taught. Teaching others my passion has led me to feel much more fulfilled in all aspects of my life.

  • The creative process brings us together as a community
  • Art helps us to overcome stress and makes us better people
  • Teaching others to be creative is one of life's most fulfilling activities
  • We value diversity and welcome everyone of all backgrounds
